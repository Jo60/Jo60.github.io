GST/HST Returns & Rebates

GST/HST filing periods may differ for some businesses, in the form of monthly, quarterly or annually. Depending on the type of your business, different methods of calculations may be required to calculate your net tax. As a result, it is important to ensure tax calculations are done correctly to minimize tax liabilities. If you are required to file a GST/HST return, you will still need to do so even if you have no business transactions or no net tax to remit.

Required:

  • Applicable Receipts and Documentation
  • HST account number, HST Access Code and filing period

WSIB Filing

The Workplace Safety and Insurance Board (WSIB) is a provincial agency that provides support and insurance for workers injured on the job. Workplaces have insurance in the events where workers get hurt while on the job so they can be assisted. To receive the appropriate benefits, a claim needs to be filed with the WSIB. We can help with registering a new account, filing a due return and calculate your due payment.

Required:

  • WSIB account number
  • Business Number and Name
  • Other Related Documentation

Canada Child Benefit (CCB) Application

The Canada Child Benefit (CCB) is a tax-free, monthly payment made to eligible families to help alleviate the cost of raising children under the age of 18. We can help with this application if you require assistance or information on how to proceed.

Old Age Security (OAS) Application

The Old Age Security (OAS) pension is a monthly payment you can receive if you are 65 and older. There are periods of times where it is more beneficial to apply for the benefit, as opposed to applying it as soon as you qualify. Regarding this, it is important to consult with our senior accountants to proceed efficiently.

  • Applicable Documentation

Canada Pension Plan (CPP) Application

The Canada Pension Plan (CPP) is a retirement pension that is a monthly, taxable benefit that replaces a portion of your income when you retire. There are periods of times where it is more beneficial to apply for the benefit, as opposed to applying it as soon as you qualify. Regarding this, it is important to consult with our senior accountants to proceed efficiently.

Disability Tax Credit (DTC) Application

The disability tax credit (DTC) is a tax credit that aims to help people with disabilities or to support people by reducing the amount of income tax that they may have to pay. It is important to understand the criteria first to see if you are eligible for the DTC. We can help with this application if you require assistance or information on how to proceed.
