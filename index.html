About Me

Introduction

Welcome to my Portfolio. I’m a freelance Front End Web Developer looking for full time employment not just in the Design feild but in Marketing and Ecommerce as well. I studied Frontend development at George Brown College. I’ve gained experience not only by freelancing but through internships as well where I learned about industry standards and best practices. My best work to date was working with a development team to design a website for a proposed University (check out the link below ) as a UI Designer.

I have Marketing experience. I did an internship as a Social Media coordinator and worked full time as a Digital Media Success Representative. I created content on client’s behalf to raise brand awareness and advertise services on Social Media. I used management platforms like Hootsuite and Later to schedule posts for optimal times. I’m familiar with some graphic design tools like Adobe Photoshop and illustrator to fix and edit photos, as well as Canva.

Photography is another passion of mine. Took an introductory course and fell in love with it. I enjoy taking photos of Landscapes and Nature like the ones you’ll see below and on my Instagram. But Ive mainly done Product photography for Instagram and Shopify accounts. Unlike most, product photography is detail oriented. It doesn’t make use of filters or editing, it relies more on proper lighting and angles to accurately depict the merchandise for customers.
