    About Me

    Welcome to my website. I’m a recent graduate from George Brown and studied both web design and development, both one year programs, and eager to begin my career in the field. I’m currently continuing to improve my skills by leaning React as well as PHP in my spare time. My best work to date was working with a development team to design a website for a proposed University (check out the link below) as a web designer.

    Photography is another passion of mine. Took an introductory course in college and fell in love with it. It’s a hobby for now but I’d like to grow my abilities to do it professionally in the future.
