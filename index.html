About Me

Introduction

I’m a Front End Web Developer looking to utilize my skills in a full-time position in the field of Web design or Marketing. I studied at George Brown College in 2016 where I learned Front-End web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and Wordpress to name a few. I continued my studies at Juno College in 2020. There I grew my skills to include React including Redux, Node.js and SASS.

I’ve gained experience not only with freelancing and internships but through contract jobs as well. During these opportunities I’ve worked while adhering to industry standards and best practices. As a frontend web developer I contributed to the design of a website built for a proposed university. I created the prototype using Bootstrap and custome CSS. Some of my work to date includes building a website for a Toronto Tax Firm from the ground up (check out the link below ) and improving a WordPress site for a Mississauga Construction Equipment Company.

In addition to web development, I have Marketing experience. I did an internship as a Social Media coordinator and worked full time as a Marketing Assistant. I created content on client’s behalf to raise brand awareness and advertise services on social media, while keeping brand consistency. I used management platforms like Hootsuite and Later to schedule posts for optimal times. As well as Canva to create posts. I’m familiar with graphic design tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to fix and edit photos.

Photography is another skill I have. Took an introductory course in college and fell in love with it. I enjoy taking photos of Natural Landscapes like the ones you’ll see below and on my Instagram account. Professionally I've done Product photography for Instagram and Shopify accounts for small businesses.
