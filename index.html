About Me

Introduction

Welcome to my Portfolio. I’m a Front End Web Developer looking for utilize my skills in a full-time position in the field of Web design, Marketing or eCommerce. I studied Web development at George Brown College. I’ve gained experience not only by freelancing and internships but through contract jobs. During these opportunities I’ve worked while adhering to industry standards and best practices. My work to date includes building a website for a Toronto Tax Firm from the ground up (check out the link below ) and optimizing a WordPress site for a Construction Equipment Company in Mississauga.

Additionally, I have experience and looking for opportunities in Marketing. I did an internship as a Social Media coordinator and worked full time as a Marketing Assistant. I created content on client’s behalf to raise brand awareness and advertise services on social media, while keeping brand consistency. I used management platforms like Hootsuite and Later to schedule posts for optimal times. As well as Canva to create posts. I've used Mailchimp to create email campaigns and graphic design tools like Adobe Photoshop to fix and edit photos.

I have over a decade of Customer Service Experience . I’m very comfortable interacting with customers face to face, over the phone and online. I’m very quick to understand their needs and I can meet those needs quickly and effectively. From my experience I’ve developed a “don’t say no” approach. In the retail or service industry when I can't offer a customer exactly what their asking, I offer some worthwhile alternatives to their request. That could mean offering a similar product we carry, special order the product they want or modifying my service to their request. Simply put, I never let the customer leave empty handed.

Photography is another passion of mine. I took an introductory course in college and fell in love with it. I enjoy taking photos of Natural Landscapes like the ones you’ll see below and on my Instagram account. But I've mainly done Product photography for Instagram and Shopify accounts for small business. Unlike most, product photography is detail oriented. It doesn’t make use of filters or editing, it relies more on proper lighting and angles to accurately depict the merchandise for customers.
